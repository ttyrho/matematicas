$F^{n}$ is the set of all lists of length $n$ of elements of $F$:

$$F^{n} = \{(x_1, \dots, x_n):x_j \in F\ for\ j = 1, \dots, n\}$$

For $(x_1, \dots, x_n). \in F$ and $j \in \{1, \dots, n\}$, we say that $x_j$ is the $j^{th}$ **coordinate** of $(x_1, \dots, x_n)$
